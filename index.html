What Happens After 21 Days?

When you’ve completed the 21-Day College Application Kickoff, you’ll be miles above the rest when it comes to preparing your applications. You’ll have completed your full school list, college resume, application theme, the common application activity list, and a brag sheet for letters of recommendation. The completed workbook and video challenges can be used even after the 21-Day College Application Kickoff is done, and you’ll leave with all the knowledge necessary to ace your applications.

You’ll also have a topic selected for your main application essay and your first draft completed. During the course, we will discuss 2 different essay formats- narrative and iterative- so you could possibly have two completed first drafts if you choose to write both.

You’ll have researched potential majors, taken 5 steps to demonstrate interest with your target schools, and identified your top personal qualities and guiding values – all of which will help you tackle your supplemental essays.
